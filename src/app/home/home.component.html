Welcome to Citco.

Citco (Cheecknal Industries and Trading Company) is a leading producer of roasted and double filtered premium coconut oil, based in Pathanamthitta, Kerala, India. Our oil is 100% pure, with no Additives and no preservatives, and holds the highest Agmark Grade 1 certification. We can supply our products within India and abroad and welcome your enquiries.

Take a look at the products we sell.

Citco products are available at many retailers around Kerala. You can also purchase directly from our factory in Omalloor, where we are always pleased to receive so many of our regular customers, both wholesale and retail.
